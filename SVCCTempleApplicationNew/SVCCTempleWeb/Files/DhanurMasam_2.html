Significance of Thiruppavai & Maargazhi(Dhanur) month
Vedas, considered to be the very breath of Parabrahmam, were given to us for conducting our day to day activities, devotion, understanding the ultimate reality and attain mukthi (salvation). Thiruppavai teaches us the same in the simple 30 pasurams and is called “vedam anaittukkum vitthu” or the “seed for all vedic knowledge”. Margazhi month is special for Hindu people which encompass more festivals and events.margazhi month is considered as divine month not only for humans and also devas.
Kindly request everyone's esteemed presence with family and friends on all weekends to participate and receive the divine blessings in the holy month of Maargazhi
